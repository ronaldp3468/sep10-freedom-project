About Inventions Used in Biochemistry

Here are some examples of inventions used in biochemistry and how they are used:

  • Electrophoresis: Used to separate and analyze proteins, nucleic acids, and other biomolecules based on their size and charge.
  • PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction): Used to amplify DNA, enabling the detection and analysis of small amounts of DNA from various sources.
  • Mass Spectrometry: Used to analyze the mass and chemical composition of molecules, including proteins, peptides, and metabolites.
